Smart City Workshop #7

A Comprehensive Research Event to Explore Cities Issues

cc by-sa, photo by DvYang

When

22 June 2013

8:30am

What

The smart city workshop's objective is to share theories, methods and tools with other researchers in an open, comprehensive point of view, with the city as a common denominator. Everyone is invited to bring his ideas about the city so we can learn from each other and discuss city issues. Come take part in an active multidisciplinary community on broad range of city based analysis.

Audience: Researchers and students from all scientific fields interested in discovering, discussing, collaborating to explore the urban space.

Where
